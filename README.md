## **The vascular mesothelium/serosa as a potential integrator of perivascular adipose tissue**

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.20397072.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20397072)

Analysis of nine single-nuclei RNA sequencing samples of mesothelial tissue from the thoracic aorta, kidney, and mesentery. Animal model is the male Dahl Salt-Sensitive (SS) rat.
